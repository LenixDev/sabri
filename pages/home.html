About Sabri Libass Fashion

Sabri Libass Fashion is a limited liability company established since 1996, located in the activity zone of Bou Saada, Wilaya of M'sila, Algeria.

We specialize in industrial production of clothing and textiles, employing 95 qualified workers. Our product lines include:

  • Children's tailored clothing and traditional garments
  • Sportswear
  • Uniforms and workwear
  • Woven and non-woven traditional attire
  • Hotel, hospital, and community textiles

After studying the Algerian market, characterized by its reliance on imports and lack of competitive local products, Sabri Libass Fashion identified a strong market opportunity. Supported by government policies encouraging local industry and export incentives, we aim to grow our presence and fully utilize our resources and expertise.

Sabri Libass Fashion serves both public and private sectors, providing tailored orders that meet specific quality standards. We have benefited from European partnerships and expert consultations that helped shape our strategic direction.

Our company has received multiple awards including first prize for the best professional product at the national traditional clothing competition in 2014, and has participated in numerous national and international exhibitions.

We continue to expand by opening branches across different wilayas and adapting our product range to meet evolving market demands, including workwear, traditional clothing, and high-quality textile products for gifts and social projects.

For more information or inquiries, feel free to contact us. We are committed to excellence and fostering strong partnerships with our clients.
